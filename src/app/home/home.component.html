How to find a nanny

  • 1

    Register online

    It’s free to register. Simply enter your name, email and password.

  • 2

    Chose nanny

    Chose from our list of nannies. You can read the comments and see the likes for every nanny.

  • 3

    Send message

    You can send a message to any nanny you have selected.

How to become a nanny

  • 1

    Register online

    It’s free to register. Simply enter your name, email and password.

  • 2

    Become a nanny

    Fill in your nanny details as description, working time, driving licence, gender and a photo.

  • 3

    Receive a message

    Parent who have chosen you will send you a message. In the menu Inbox, you can read your messages.
